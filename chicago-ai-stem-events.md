### 1. TTIC (Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago)

TTIC runs approximately 3 public talks per week across multiple series. All are in TTIC Room 530 (6045 S. Kenwood Ave, inside the UChicago campus).

#### TTIC Colloquium (Weekly)
- **Organizer:** TTIC
- **Frequency:** Weekly (typically Mondays)
- **Time:** 11:30am
- **Location:** TTIC Room 530, 6045 S. Kenwood Ave, Chicago
- **URL:** https://www.ttic.edu/colloquium/
- **Cost:** Free, open to public
- **Description:** Forum for invited speakers on work of current relevance and broad interest to the CS community. Contact: Zhiyuan Li (zhiyuanli@ttic.edu)

#### TTIC Young Researcher Seminar Series (Weekly)
- **Organizer:** TTIC
- **Frequency:** Weekly (typically Wednesdays)
- **Time:** 11:00am
- **Location:** TTIC Room 530
- **URL:** https://www.ttic.edu/young-researcher/
- **Cost:** Free, open to public
- **Description:** Talks by PhD students and postdocs whose research is of broad interest. Opportunity for early-career researchers to present and meet TTIC/UChicago community. Contact: Shiry Ginosar (shiry@ttic.edu)

#### Research at TTIC Seminar Series (Weekly)
- **Organizer:** TTIC
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Time:** Varies (check calendar)
- **Location:** TTIC Room 530
- **URL:** https://www.ttic.edu/research-at-ttic/

## NOTES

- Most university seminars (TTIC, UChicago, Northwestern) are technically open to the public but attendees are expected to be researchers/students/professionals. No one will turn you away.
- TTIC is your best bet for consistent, high-quality, almost-daily CS/AI talks in one location.
- 1871 has the highest volume of events (1000+/year) but quality varies -- check each listing.
- For the most up-to-date schedule, subscribe to CHI IRL texts and check TTIC/UChicago/Northwestern calendars weekly.
- Fermilab and Argonne require advance registration and are ~30-45 min drive from downtown Chicago.
